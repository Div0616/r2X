How Much Does a Website Cost in India in 2026?

By Divyanshu Singh · March 2026 · 5 min read

Whether you're a startup founder, a local business owner, or a freelancer trying to get online — the first question you'll Google is always the same. Here's the honest answer nobody else will give you.

Why Website Pricing in India Is So Confusing

Search for "website design cost India" and you'll get answers ranging from ₹5,000 to ₹5,00,000. Both are technically correct. The difference is in what you're actually getting — and understanding that gap is the most important thing before you hire anyone.

The Four Price Ranges (And What You Actually Get)

₹5,000 – ₹15,000 — Template Sites

At this range, you're usually getting a pre-built template dropped on WordPress or Wix with your logo slapped on. It works, technically. But it won't look like you, won't load particularly fast, and won't be built to grow. Fine for testing an idea. Not fine for building a brand.

₹15,000 – ₹50,000 — Custom Designed Websites

This is where real work starts. A good freelance studio (like us) will design your site from scratch — custom layouts, proper mobile responsiveness, your brand colours, and clean code. For most small businesses and personal brands, this is the sweet spot.

₹50,000 – ₹2,00,000 — E-Commerce & Web Applications

If you need a store with inventory, payments, and user accounts — or a web app with a backend — you're in this range. You're paying for real engineering: databases, APIs, security, and scale.

₹2,00,000+ — Enterprise & Complex Systems

Large agencies, complex platforms, and multinational brands live here. Usually overkill for small-to-medium businesses unless you have very specific scale requirements.

What Affects the Price?

  • Number of pages — A 3-page portfolio costs less than a 15-page service site.
  • Custom design vs template — The biggest single factor.
  • Animations and interactions — Smooth scroll effects, micro-interactions, and motion all add time.
  • Backend requirements — Contact forms are cheap. Full dashboards are not.
  • E-commerce features — Payment gateways, product management, cart logic.
  • SEO setup — Some studios include it, many don't. Always ask.

What r2X Studio Charges

Roughly: portfolio sites start from ₹15,000. Business websites from ₹25,000. E-commerce from ₹60,000. Everything includes mobile-first design, basic SEO setup, and a handoff that you can actually manage.

The Mistake Most People Make

They optimise for the cheapest option and pay twice — once for the cheap site, again to fix or redo it when it doesn't work. A website is the only salesperson who works 24/7 without a salary. Invest in it accordingly.
