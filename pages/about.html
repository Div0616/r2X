The Team

Two minds,
one vision.

Every great studio is built on the chemistry between people. At r2X, that chemistry is our greatest asset — two designers, one shared obsession with extraordinary work.

Riya Mehta
Creative Director & Co-founder

Riya brings a decade of experience turning abstract briefs into visual languages that resonate. Her background spans editorial design, brand strategy, and motion — she's the one who asks "why does this exist?" before ever opening a new file. She believes the best design makes people feel something before they can explain why.
